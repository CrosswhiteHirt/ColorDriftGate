Privacy Policy

Color Drift Gate

Effective date: June 24, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Color Drift Gate handles information. The game is designed as an offline puzzle experience with local progress storage and ad-supported monetization.

Developer Contact

Developer account and support email: hengbozail@163.com.

Information Stored on Your Device

Color Drift Gate stores gameplay and preference data locally on your device, including completed levels, best star ratings, and music or sound settings. This information is used to restore your progress and settings. The game does not require account registration.

Advertising and App Tracking Transparency

The app may show rewarded or interstitial advertising through third-party advertising SDKs. Before advertising SDK setup, the app requests permission through Apple's App Tracking Transparency framework when required by iOS. If you deny tracking permission, the game still works, and ads may be less personalized.

The current app build includes advertising SDK integrations for Chartboost, Yandex Mobile Ads, Vungle, and InMobi. The app retrieves dynamic ad configuration for the app bundle identifier and only activates supported ad platforms when valid configuration is returned.

Device Identifiers

If tracking permission is granted, the app may access the Identifier for Advertisers (IDFA) for advertising purposes. The app may also use Apple's Identifier for Vendor (IDFV) when initializing security-related services. These identifiers are handled according to Apple's platform rules and the policies of the relevant SDK providers.

Security SDK

The app includes HSSecurity.framework. After the tracking permission flow finishes, the app initializes this security SDK on supported device builds. The SDK is used for app security and integrity-related functions.

Third-Party Services

Third-party advertising and security SDKs may process device, advertising, diagnostic, or interaction data according to their own privacy policies. These services may use network access to request ad configuration, load ads, report ad delivery, prevent abuse, or support SDK functionality.

Children's Privacy

Color Drift Gate does not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe that a child has provided personal information through the app, contact us so we can review the issue.

Your Choices

  • You can allow or deny tracking permission in the iOS App Tracking Transparency prompt.
  • You can change tracking permission later in iOS Settings.
  • You can delete the app to remove locally stored game progress and settings from the device.

Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated when app features, SDK integrations, or legal requirements change. The effective date above will be updated when a new version is published.
