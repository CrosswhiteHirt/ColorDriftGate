Dropwright Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 24, 2026

Developer contact: hengbozail@163.com

Overview

Dropwright is an offline physics puzzle game. Core gameplay, level progress, settings, audio preferences, and stars are stored locally on your device so the game can remember your progress and preferences.

This policy explains what information may be processed by the app and by integrated third-party services used for advertising, app security, and related technical operation.

Information Stored on Your Device

The app may store the following information locally:

  • Current level, unlocked levels, stars, and puzzle progress.
  • Music, sound, vibration, and other game settings.
  • Temporary gameplay state used for retrying or adjusting a level.

This local gameplay data is used only to operate the game experience on your device.

Advertising and Tracking Permission

Dropwright includes advertising SDK integrations. The app asks for App Tracking Transparency permission before accessing the device advertising identifier on supported iOS versions. If you allow tracking, the advertising identifier may be used to deliver and measure ads. If you decline, the app continues to work, and ads may be less personalized.

The app code includes integrations for Chartboost, Yandex Mobile Ads, Vungle Ads, and InMobi SDK. These third-party providers may process information such as advertising identifiers, device information, app interactions, ad impressions, ad clicks, and network information according to their own privacy policies.

Ad Configuration Requests

The app may request advertising configuration from a remote endpoint using the app bundle identifier. This allows the app to decide which supported ad platforms and ad units are active. The request is used for ad setup and does not contain your puzzle progress.

Security SDK

Dropwright includes HSSecurity for app security and integrity support. On supported device builds, this SDK may use technical identifiers such as the app bundle identifier and vendor identifier to initialize security services and help protect the app environment.

Analytics and Diagnostics

Third-party SDKs included for advertising or app operation may collect diagnostic, performance, crash, or event data as part of their services. Dropwright does not require you to create an account and does not ask you to provide a name, email address, phone number, or address inside the game.

Children's Privacy

Dropwright is a general-audience game and does not knowingly request personal information directly from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information through support contact, please email the developer so the information can be deleted.

Data Sharing

The developer does not sell personal information directly. Information may be processed by third-party advertising, security, and technical service providers as described above, where those services are integrated into the app.

Your Choices

  • You can allow or deny tracking through the iOS App Tracking Transparency prompt.
  • You can change tracking permission later in iOS Settings.
  • You can reset local game progress from the app if the feature is available in your version.
  • You can delete the app to remove local game data from your device.

Contact

For privacy questions, support requests, or deletion requests related to information you sent directly by email, contact: hengbozail@163.com.

Policy Updates
