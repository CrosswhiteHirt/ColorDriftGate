Color Drift Gate

Swipe blocks toward colorful gates, clear matching edges, and solve each timed puzzle before the board fills your focus.

About the Game

Color Drift Gate is a portrait color puzzle built for quick, readable play. Each level gives you an irregular board of colored blocks and four directional gates. Swipe toward a gate to push the board in that direction and absorb matching edge blocks.

The challenge comes from choosing the right direction at the right moment. Later levels add more blocks, more colors, and tighter timers while keeping the core rule easy to understand.

Features

  • Swipe-based color clearing with directional gates.
  • Irregular puzzle boards with empty spaces and edge-focused decisions.
  • Timed levels with star ratings based on remaining time.
  • Offline progress saving for completed levels and best stars.
  • Music and sound controls for comfortable play.
  • Designed for iPhone and iPad in portrait orientation.
